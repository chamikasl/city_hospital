About Our Hospital


City Hospitals is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional medical care. With a team of highly skilled doctors and state-of-the-art facilities, we offer comprehensive healthcare services to meet all your needs. Our experienced doctors stay updated with the latest advancements in medicine to provide you with personalized treatment plans. We prioritize your well-being, ensuring a patient-centered approach where your concerns and preferences are heard and respected. Quality and safety are our top priorities, and we maintain strict protocols to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. At City Hospitals, we believe in collaboration and teamwork, working together to deliver comprehensive and coordinated care. We are committed to empowering you with information so that you can actively participate in your healthcare journey.


Our hospital network has multiple branches conveniently located in Jaffna, Galle, and Kurunegala. Each location is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals ready to provide you with the highest standard of care. Whether you require routine check-ups, specialized treatments, or diagnostic services, our hospitals are well-equipped to cater to your healthcare needs. With a commitment to excellence, compassion, and patient-centric care, City Hospitals is your trusted healthcare partner.
